Welcome

This HR API expose the following endpoints:

  • List of all employees: host/employees
  • Employees filtered by gender(ex: M, F or undefined): host/employees/?gender=m
  • Employee by id: host/employee/3
  • Average age per industry: host/avg_age_industry
  • Average salaries per industry: host/avg_salary_industry
  • Average salaries per years of experience: host/avg_salary_experience
  • Total of salaries: host/total_salary
  • Total of salaries per industry: host/sallary_per_industry
  • Employees over age 60: host/employees_over_60
